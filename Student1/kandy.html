Kandy

Kandy is Sri Lanka's second largest city after
Colombo. Kandy is both an administrative and
religious city in Central Province.Kandy is a
sinhalese majority city; there are sizable
communities belonging to other ethnic groups,
such as Moors and Tamils.
Kandy is the home of the Temple of Tooth Relic
(Sri Dalada Maligawa).And it is the most sacred
place of worship in the buddhist world.It was
declared a World Heritage Site by UNESCO in 1988.
Not only Temple of Tooth Relic there are many
places like Peradeniya Botanical Garden ,
Gadaladeniya Viharaya , Lankathilaka Temple
and etc to visit in Kandy.
The city and the region have been known by many
different names.However, the more popular historical
name is Senkadagala or Senkadagalapura, officially
Senkadagala Siriwardhana Maha Nuwara (meaning
'great city of Senkadagala of growing resplendence'),
generally shortened to 'Maha Nuwara'.
